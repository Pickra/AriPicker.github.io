Politi-Troll

I wanted an easy way to look up politician's voting records. This information is difficult to find even for an educated person. In order to hold our representatives more accountable, I wanted to make something that would be usable even for a layman. I built it in Backbone, with Yeoman and SCSS. I used JSONP to retrieve data from the govtrack api. This is still in development. At the moment, you can pull down the 10 latest votes for each politician, and when and how they voted. Politician's vote on thousands, sometimes tens of thousands, of things during their terms. In order for the user to not be overwhelmed by this immense amount of data, my next step is to add a keyword search functinality that filters down the votes with each new keyword. Here is the corresponding github repo.
